Bio in Brief

Rohan Aslam is a 2nd year student currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science and CCIT at the University of Toronto. During his studies, Rohan has gained hands-on experience through a internship at RBC, where he worked on various web development/design projects as a developer. In his free time, Rohan enjoys staying up-to-date with the latest technologies and trends in the field of web and software development.

In addition to his technical skills, Rohan is a team player who is known for his strong work ethic and attention to detail. He is passionate about using technology to solve real-world problems and is always looking for opportunities to learn and grow.

Through his internships and coursework, Rohan has gained expertise in a range of web development technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React. Moreover, he has gained experience in design especially UI/UX and Product design. He is always eager to take on new challenges and is committed to continuously improving his skills.

In the future, Rohan hopes to use his knowledge and experience to build and design innovative technologies that make a positive impact on the world.
