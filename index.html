Bio in Brief

Rohan Aslam is a student currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science and CCIT at the University of Toronto Mississauga. During his studies, Rohan has gained hands-on experience through internships at RBC, where he worked on various web development projects as a developer. In his free time, Rohan enjoys staying up-to-date with the latest technologies and trends in the field of web and software development.

In addition to his technical skills, Rohan is a team player who is known for his strong work ethic and attention to detail. He is passionate about using technology to solve real-world problems and is always looking for opportunities to learn and grow.

Through his internships and coursework, Rohan has gained expertise in a range of web development technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React. He is always eager to take on new challenges and is committed to continuously improving his skills.

In the future, Rohan hopes to use his knowledge and experience to build innovative web applications that make a positive impact on the world.
